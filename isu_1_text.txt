Synopsis 
Dr.Ponter Boddit is a physicist in the parallel version of earth where Neanderthals have become the dominant species. Ponter came to work to his Quantum Computing experiment  in a mining facility in Sudbury Ontario.  While the startup procedures of the intricate quantum computer Ponter had to go fix a slightly malfunctioning part which resulted in him being teleported into our version of hearth inside a tank of heavy water.
